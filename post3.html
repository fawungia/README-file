Layering Actives

If you've wandered down a skincare aisle recently, you’ve probably seen the word "Peptides" plastered across every other jar. They are undoubtedly the buzziest ingredient right now. But what actually are they, and more importantly, do they live up to the clinical hype?

Peptides are tiny fragments of proteins. Think of your skin as a brick wall; collagen and elastin are the mortar holding everything together. As we age, that mortar breaks down. Peptides send signals to your cells, "tricking" them into producing more collagen to fix the perceived damage.
