5 Affordable Skincare Products

By Editor • June 22 5 Min Read

There is a massive misconception in the beauty world that you have to spend a car payment on a tiny jar of cream to get glowing skin. Consistently using effective basics is the real secret to barrier health.

I test a lot of formulations for this journal, and I’m always thrilled when a budget-friendly option clinically performs better than a luxury one. Here are five affordable products that have genuinely earned their permanent spot.

1. The Hydrating Cleanser

You don't need expensive actives in a product you're going to wash down the drain in 60 seconds.

2. Pure Squalane Oil

Squalane is a skin-identical lipid that provides incredible hydration without being heavy or greasy. Many drugstore versions for under $15 work perfectly.

3. Mineral Sunscreen

High-quality zinc oxide doesn't have to be pricey. Many drugstore formulations now offer elegant finishes.

The Bottom Line: Don't let the price tag fool you. Read the labels and understand the ingredients.
