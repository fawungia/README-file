Gentle Exfoliation

By Editor • June 20 5 Min Read

A few years ago, I fell victim to the "tingle means it's working" myth. I was using a gritty face scrub, a strong acid toner, and a weekly chemical peel. Instead of polished perfection, my face was constantly red and tight.

I had completely destroyed my skin barrier. Here is the honest truth about exfoliation: your skin already knows how to shed dead skin cells. We are meant to gently assist the skin, not forcefuly strip it away.

My New Rules:

  • Ditch physical scrubs: If I want physical texture, I just use a soft washcloth.
  • Switch to gentle chemical exfoliants: I swapped strong acids for milder Lactic Acid twice a week.
  • Never mix actives: If it’s my night to exfoliate, I skip the retinoids.

If your skin is stinging, step away from the acids. Be kind to your face.
