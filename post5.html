Nightly Rituals: Maximizing the Skin's Repair Phase.

By Sarah • July 10, 2026 6 Min Read

While you sleep, your skin goes into "overdrive" mode. Blood flow to the skin increases, and it focuses on repairing the damage done by UV rays and pollution during the day. This is the time to use your most powerful actives and deepest hydrators.

The Double Cleanse

I always start with an oil-based cleanser to melt away the day's SPF and pollution, followed by a gentle water-based cleanser. You cannot repair skin that still has a layer of urban grime sitting on top of it.

Active Treatment

This is where I rotate my "power players." One night it might be a Retinoid for cellular turnover, the next night a Peptide serum for remodeling. Never overload your skin with too many actives at once.

Finally, I seal everything in with a rich, ceramide-heavy night cream. Wake up with skin that feels rested, even if your brain doesn't.
