Privacy Policy & Disclosures

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to SkinHonestly. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us or that is collected automatically via cookies:

  • Email Addresses: When you sign up for our newsletter or free PDF guides, we collect your email address and name.
  • Contact Forms: When you reach out to us via our Contact page, we collect the data you submit to respond to your inquiry.
  • Usage Data: We may use standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to see which pages are popular so we can create better skincare content. This data is anonymous.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to provide a better experience on SkinHonestly. We will use your email to send you the resources you requested, weekly skincare tips, and occasional updates about our products. We will never sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

3. FTC Affiliate Disclosure

SkinHonestly believes in total transparency. Some of the links on this website are affiliate links. This means that if you click on the link and purchase an item, we may receive a small affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely use, trust, and believe will bring value to our readers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to help run our website, such as email marketing platforms (e.g., MailerLite) to send our newsletters. These providers have their own strict privacy policies and only use your information as directed by us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to opt out of our email communications at any time. Every email we send includes an "Unsubscribe" link at the very bottom. If you would like us to delete your data completely, please contact us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us via the Contact Form on our About page.
