The Truth About Eye Creams

By Editor • June 17 5 Min Read

The beauty industry loves to convince us that if we don't have a highly specific product for every square inch of our faces, we are doing something wrong. But do you actually need a separate eye cream?

The Short Answer

For most people? No. A solid, fragrance-free facial moisturizer is perfectly capable of keeping your under-eyes protected.

When Should You Buy One?

Only if you are trying to treat a specific under-eye issue:

  • Dark Circles & Puffiness: You need an eye cream with caffeine.
  • Deep Crow's Feet: You need a specialized retinol eye cream.

The Bottom Line: If your current routine is working, save your money!
