My Honest Morning Routine: What I Use Every Single Day.

By Sarah • June 23, 2026 5 Min Read

I’ll be the first to admit it: my skincare routine used to be exhausting. I thought I needed a ten-step process to achieve that elusive "glass skin," but in reality, all I got was an empty wallet and skin that felt overwhelmed and congested. Over the last year, I’ve drastically simplified my mornings.

I’ve realized that when it comes to the start of the day, protection and hydration are the only true goals. Here is the honest, paired-down routine I actually stick to:

Step 1: A Gentle Splash

Unless I slugged with a heavy ointment the night before, I usually skip morning cleanser. A gentle splash of lukewarm water is all my skin needs. It leaves my natural moisture barrier intact and preps my face for the next steps.

Step 2: Vitamin C

Once my face is patted dry, I apply a few drops of a Vitamin C serum. This is my non-negotiable antioxidant shield against daily pollution and sun damage. It’s lightweight and essential for that morning radiance.

Step 3: The Daily Reset Serum

While my skin is still slightly damp, I go in with two pumps of our very own SkinHonestly Daily Reset Serum. I formulated this specifically for this step. It’s packed with pure hydration that locks in moisture without feeling tacky.

Step 4: Moisturize and Protect

Finally, I lock it all in. If it’s winter, I’ll use a lightweight ceramide moisturizer. But in the warmer months? I just use a moisturizing SPF 50. My absolute favorite sheer mineral SPF doubles as my moisturizer.

And that’s it! Four steps, zero stress. Let's keep it honest—what does your morning look like?
