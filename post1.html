My Honest Morning Routine: What I Use Every Single Day

Published: June 23, 2026

I’ll be the first to admit it: my skincare routine used to be exhausting. I thought I needed a ten-step process to achieve that elusive "glass skin," but in reality, all I got was an empty wallet and skin that felt overwhelmed and congested.

Here is my honest, paired-down morning routine that I actually stick to every single day:

Step 1: A Gentle Splash

Unless I slugged with a heavy ointment the night before, I usually skip morning cleanser. A gentle splash of lukewarm water is all my skin needs. It leaves my natural moisture barrier intact.

Step 2: Vitamin C

Once my face is patted dry, I apply a few drops of a Vitamin C serum. This is my non-negotiable antioxidant shield against daily pollution and sun damage.

Step 3: The Daily Reset Serum

While my skin is still slightly damp, I go in with two pumps of our very own SkinHonestly Daily Reset Serum. It’s packed with pure, uncomplicated hydration that locks in moisture.

Step 4: Moisturize and Protect

Finally, I lock it all in. I just use a moisturizing SPF 50. My absolute favorite sheer mineral SPF 50 doubles as my morning moisturizer.

And that’s it! Four steps, zero stress.
