My Honest Morning Routine: What I Use Every Single Day.

By Sarah • June 23, 2026

I’ll be the first to admit it: my skincare routine used to be exhausting. I thought I needed a ten-step process to achieve that elusive "glass skin," but in reality, all I got was an empty wallet and skin that felt overwhelmed and congested. Over the last year, I’ve drastically simplified my mornings.

I’ve realized that when it comes to the start of the day, protection and hydration are the only true goals. Here is the honest, paired-down routine I actually stick to:

Step 1: A Gentle Splash

Unless I used a heavy occlusive overnight, I usually skip morning cleanser. A gentle splash of lukewarm water is all my skin needs to remain balanced, leaving my natural moisture barrier intact.

Step 2: Vitamin C

Once my face is patted dry, I apply a few drops of a Vitamin C serum. This is my non-negotiable antioxidant shield against daily pollution and environmental stressors.

Step 3: The Daily Reset Serum

While my skin is still slightly damp, I go in with two pumps of our SkinHonestly Daily Reset Serum. It’s formulated specifically to lock in hydration and support the lipid barrier.

Step 4: Moisturize and Protect

Finally, I lock it all in. My absolute favorite is a sheer mineral SPF 50. It serves as both my moisturizer and my primary defense against UV rays.
