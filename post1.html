My Honest Morning Routine: What I Use Every Single Day

Published: June 23, 2026

I’ll be the first to admit it: my skincare routine used to be exhausting. I thought I needed a ten-step process to achieve that elusive "glass skin," but in reality, all I got was an empty wallet and skin that felt overwhelmed and congested. Over the last year, I’ve drastically simplified my mornings. I’ve realized that when it comes to the start of the day, protection and hydration are the only true goals.

Here is my honest, paired-down morning routine that I actually stick to every single day:

Step 1: A Gentle Splash

Unless I slugged with a heavy ointment the night before, I usually skip morning cleanser. A gentle splash of lukewarm water is all my skin needs. It leaves my natural moisture barrier intact and preps my face for the next steps.

Step 2: Vitamin C

Once my face is patted dry, I apply a few drops of a Vitamin C serum. This is my non-negotiable antioxidant shield against daily pollution and sun damage. I’m currently linking my favorite, wallet-friendly Vitamin C serum over on the Shop page—it’s lightweight and doesn’t smell like hot dog water (iykyk).

Step 3: The Daily Reset Serum

While my skin is still slightly damp from the Vitamin C, I go in with two pumps of our very own SkinHonestly Daily Reset Serum. I formulated this specifically for this step in the routine. It’s packed with pure, uncomplicated hydration that locks in moisture without feeling tacky. It plays so beautifully under makeup, too.

Step 4: Moisturize and Protect

Finally, I lock it all in. If it’s the dead of winter, I’ll use a lightweight ceramide moisturizer followed by sunscreen. But in the warmer months? I just use a moisturizing SPF 50. My absolute favorite sheer mineral SPF 50 is on my recommended list—it leaves zero white cast and doubles as my morning moisturizer.

And that’s it! Four steps, zero stress. What does your morning routine look like these days? Let's keep it honest!
