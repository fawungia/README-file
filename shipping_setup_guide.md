# Shopify + CJ Dropshipping Shipping Setup Guide

## Step 1: Connect CJ to Shopify
1. Go to CJ Dropshipping > Authorization > Shopify.
2. Click "Add Store" and enter your Shopify URL.
3. Click "Authorize" and install the app in Shopify.

## Step 2: Set up Shipping Zones in Shopify
1. Shopify Settings > Shipping and Delivery > General > Manage.
2. **Create Zone 1 (Free Shipping Club)**:
   - Name: "Free Shipping Countries"
   - Countries: USA, UK, Canada, Australia.
   - Add Rate: Name "Free Standard Shipping", Price: 0.
3. **Create Zone 2 (Paid Shipping Club)**:
   - Name: "International Shipping"
   - Countries: Rest of World.
   - Add Rate: Name "Standard International Shipping", Price: $9.99.
4. Click **SAVE**.

## Step 3: Verify Fulfillment Location
1. Shopify Settings > Locations.
2. Ensure "CJdropshipping" is listed as a location.

## Step 4: Set Default Shipping in CJ
1. My CJ > Settings > Shipping Method.
2. Set "CJ Packet Ordinary" (or preferred) as default.
3. Confirm.

## Pro Tip: Pricing
Always include the shipping cost in your product price to offer "Free Shipping" to customers.
Formula: (Product Cost + CJ Shipping Cost) * 2 or 3 = Your Selling Price.
