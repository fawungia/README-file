The Science of Hyaluronic Acid

By Editor • July 05 5 Min Read

Hyaluronic acid (HA) is often hailed as the "holy grail" of skin hydration. But while HA is a powerful humectant, the way you use it matters more than the percentage in the bottle.

How to Use it Correcty

If you apply HA to bone-dry skin, it can actually pull moisture out of your skin. Always apply HA to slightly damp skin and immediately seal it with an occlusive moisturizer.
